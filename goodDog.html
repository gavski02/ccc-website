Good Dog TT - June 2nd

We're absolutely pumped about this addition to the CCC. Bend's newest paved bike path parallels Century Drive, running from the newly Branded Loge Entrada Lodge on the southwestern edge of town to the new Deschutes National Forest Visitors Center. Just under 5 kilometers of one-lane path, with just 150 feet of gradual ascent, this prologue style time trial course is sure to blow out the legs going into the weekend. Importantly, this race will be Merckx style, with equipment restrictions on any component that is not mass-start legal.
