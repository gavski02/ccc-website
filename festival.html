Featuring an entirely new venue for us, the Sul Fiume ("on the river") Cycling Festival will be a full-scale event on the banks of the Deschutes River. The schedule of events is designed to bring excitement for the whole family in tandem with the criterium racing action at the 2019 Cascade Cycling Classic. The day begins with the Cortile Gravel Grinder, taking advantage of the “backyard” trails and roads we love around Bend. Riders will follow a crumb trail of pink ribbons marking the course, leading to adventure and surprise, which has always been one of our favorite ways to enjoy riding bicycles. After the ride, watch the little ones find their spark on the Strider-dome course. Don’t have a Strider bike for your 2-6 year old kiddo? Demo a Cascade Cycling Classic Youth Foundation Strider complete with a helmet and a guaranteed big smile. Continue the stoke by cheering on the middle schoolers in the Middle School Grand Prix, a fun team event inspired by the "Little 500," on part of the criterium race course. Throughout the day and evening you can enjoy refreshing Worthy Brewing beer, delicious food from many vendors, and high energy groovy tunes by several favorite local bands. Don't forget to check out our cycling expo with many great companies that love making products for those who love to ride. And finally, get ready as the close-quarters racing action of the Pro Men and Pro Women heats up. Watch them take on an all-new fast, technical, riverside course that is sure to leave your heart pumping well into the evening!
