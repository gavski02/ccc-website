Though the dates and race routes have changed the Cascade Cycling Classic remains the longest consecutively run elite stage race in the country because of the support of volunteers like YOU!

Your gift of time and energy continue to keep the CCC a safe and fun event for the athletes and community. Please consider being part of this re-vamped and epic event by clicking on the Sign Up link and looking for a slot that fits your schedule.

If you have questions please feel free to email, text, or call our Volunteer Director, Anne Codding,(541.520.9455 anne@cascade-classic.org) with any questions. She does her best to respond to inquiries within 24 hours.

We will see you in May!
