HOUSING HOSTS
Do you want to volunteer as a host family to house athletes and support team members? Do you have space in your home?

Dates for the 2019 Cascade Cycling Classic are May 29 â€“ June 2.

Athletes usually arrive a day or two before the race and leave the last day of racing or the day after. They will be expected to communicate with you directly after Becky places them.

As a host family, your obligation opens your home to your level of comfort for one or several race participants. Some cyclists will be traveling with support staff that we try to keep with the team. Please be sure that you have enough beds for the number of cyclists you will be hosting; sometimes support members are fine sleeping on a couch or even an air mattress on the floor. It is important that you be able to provide racers access to the kitchen to store and prepare food, as well as have a safe place to store their bikes. Anything you wish to provide above and beyond these basic accommodations to make their stay more comfortable is entirely up to you.

As the race gets closer and more racers request host housing, Becky will match cyclists with housing appropriate to their team size. All housing requests will be matched with host families by Wednesday, May 15, 2019. Team sizes can range from 8 racers (plus potential staff members) down to single riders or groups of 2. If you know of a neighbor or neighbors who might be interested in hosting, this enables us to keep large teams in the same neighborhood, as they often arrive in 1 vehicle.

If you are interested in hosting athletes for this long-standing community event, please download and fill out the 2019 Host Housing Volunteer Form PDF and return it to Becky at ccchousing@cascade-classic.org Also feel free to share this link with others who might also be interested in being a host!

Thank you for your support! We look forward to working with you.
