Stage 4: Sul Fiume Cycling Festival Crit - June 1st

A huge departure from the Downtown Bend criterium that the CCC has long featured as it's showcase stage for the weekend, an entirely new venue on the banks of the Deschutes River will allow us to host a full-scale cycling festival in tandem with the criterium racing action. This change will feature more engaging terrain to mix up the most exciting, close quarters racing action of the weekend, plus an all new Strider race for the kiddos and a separate group ride start for all on some of our favorite gravel roads outside of Bend. The all new High school grand prix will also be featured on this day, running "Little 500" style on a part of the closed criterium race course. And in the middle of it all, we'll be featuring live music and a cycling expo to showcase some of the many great new events and products in the cycling industry. We're incredibly excited for this new addition in that it will allow for a far more inclusive, all-encompassing race atmosphere.
