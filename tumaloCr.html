Stage 1: Tumalo Circuit Race - May 29th

For the first time in years, the CCC is reintroducing some of the outstanding gravel roads just outside of Bend. Approximately 4 miles of this 10 mile circuit, broken into two sectors, will be featured in the fast-paced opening stage of the 2019 CCC. Mostly flat, the only real separators on this course will be the inherent mechanical risk in racing on gravel roads and the fairly technical nature of one particular corner on the longer gravel sector off of Bend's infamous Sisemore Road.
